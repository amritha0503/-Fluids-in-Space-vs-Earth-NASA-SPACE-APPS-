Key Scientific Topics

Surface Tension

The cohesive force between liquid molecules at the surface

Examples:
  • Water droplets forming spheres
  • Insects walking on water
  • Capillary action in plants

Microgravity

Environment where gravitational force is greatly reduced

Examples:
  • International Space Station orbit
  • Parabolic flight experiments
  • Drop tower facilities

Marangoni Effect

Mass transfer along an interface due to surface tension gradients

Examples:
  • Wine tears on glass
  • Crystal growth in space
  • Welding defects prevention

Colloidal Systems

Mixtures where particles (1-1000nm) are dispersed in a medium

Examples:
  • Milk and fog
  • Paints and inks
  • Blood and gelatin
